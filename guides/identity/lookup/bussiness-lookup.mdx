**PERFORM** **BATCH LOOKUP**

Customers with an extensive amount of user data aiming to authenticate their business identities through Business Lookup can utilize the Business Batch Lookup feature by following the outlined process:

1) Customers can initiate a Business Batch Lookup by clicking on the "Perform Batch Lookup" button, prompting the **side modal** to appear, as illustrated in the image below;

![](https://guides.dojah.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3296995301-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F2pgHlRJB7EejHn55UcwX%252Fuploads%252FhqDDHDMFDegutcrL3SoP%252FScreenshot%25202024-06-03%2520at%252010.02.51.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3Dff52b8a5-3a5f-4e37-ac35-6666adbd9f79&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=b5ad4983&sv=1)

Side modal for performing a batch lookup

2) Customers are obligated to select an **app** to use for the verification process.

![](https://guides.dojah.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3296995301-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F2pgHlRJB7EejHn55UcwX%252Fuploads%252Fvt3LAhroIOtzI8PAd0Hz%252FScreenshot%25202024-06-03%2520at%252010.10.36.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D855c1675-72eb-4d06-96ab-82b0d434bfe7&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=ae0e99d&sv=1)

Select an App

3) Customers need to choose an **ID type**, and this choice is contingent on the selected country. After selecting the ID type, it prompts the option to **upload the batch** file either in their format or download **the template** by clicking on "Click here to select." They can then populate the template and re-upload it.

![](https://guides.dojah.io/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F3296995301-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F2pgHlRJB7EejHn55UcwX%252Fuploads%252F3pBkm3QrxRb8OcfbqYiX%252FScreenshot%25202024-06-03%2520at%252010.12.04.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D5576f2a2-4454-447b-9df1-3ebccc9f7596&width=768&dpr=4&quality=100&sign=7c786dd1&sv=1)

Select ID type and either upload a batch file or download template then populate it with users data
