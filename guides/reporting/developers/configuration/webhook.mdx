Webhooks allow you to receive real-time notifications about events happening on your Dojah account.

**To set up webhooks, follow these steps:**

1.  Go to the Dojah dashboard and click on the **Webhooks** tab.
    
2.  Click on the **Add a webhook URL** button.
    
3.  Enter the URL of your application that you want to receive notifications from Dojah.
    
4.  Select the events that you want to receive notifications about.
    
5.  Click on the **Create Webhook** button.
